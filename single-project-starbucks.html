Christmas Instagram Promo

Starbucks

Toghether with a photographer we created an animated Instagram reel for the Starbucks Coffee Limited Edition. Our goal was to emphasize the spirit of Christmas while remaining close to the brand's identity. Festive props and animated characters help showcase the products of the Limited Edition. The interaction between the characters and the product brings playfulness and joy to the scene.

We made product photography for the Christmas scene and the individual product items, then retouched and cut out the details for further processing in Adobe After Effects. The characters were carefully animated. The Starbucks logo was designed in Cinema 4D.
