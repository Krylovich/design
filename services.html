Mixed Media motion graphic designer

Hi there! My name is Yana and I am a freelance motion graphic designer and animator based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. I specialize in creating dynamic, visually-stunning animations using a range of techniques and styles.

In addition to my work, I am also an avid fan of indie rock music and the Early Netherlandish painting school. I draw inspiration from the creative energy of both mediums and strive to bring that same level of innovation and expression to my animations.

One of my biggest passions is working with collage and mixed media animation. For me, this approach is not just about combining different images and videos together, but rather using a variety of techniques and approaches to achieve a unique and captivating end result. Whether it's drawing frame-by-frame, modeling objects in 3D, scanning real-world objects, or adding stop motion transitions, I believe there are no limits to the tools and techniques that can be used to bring animation to life.
