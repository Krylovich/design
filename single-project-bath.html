Advertising Animation

Bath&Body Works

An advertising campaign for Bath&Body Works Aromatherapy body care products. The project is a compilation of product photography and collage animation of watercolor illustrations. The aim was to increase brand awareness via social media platforms.

The watercolor illustrations were carefully drawn by me and then scanned. The videos were made in Adobe After Effects.
